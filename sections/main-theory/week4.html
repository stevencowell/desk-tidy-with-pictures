Week 4: Planning and Materials

With your design complete, this week focuses on planning the build and selecting suitable timber. Pine is often used because it is a softwood that cuts easily and is inexpensive. Check what sizes are available and adjust your design if needed. Make a simple plan that lists each piece and the order you will cut, drill and join them.
